### Mobile phone number  (with - or . or space) :

The number can be this format (and variations with . or , or - space)

- xxxxxxxx
- +225xxxxxxxx
- (+225)xxxxxxxx
- (00225)xxxxxxxx
<br>
<br>
  
- xx.xx.xx.xx
- (+225)xx.xx.xx.xx
- +225xx.xx.xx.xx
<br>
<br>

- xx xx xx xx
- (+225)xx xx xx xx
- +225 xx xx xx xx
